Orlando Perl Mongers

Welcome

Welcome to the Orlando Perl Mongers website. We're currently undergoing some re-organization as our regular meeting attendance fell off sharply over the last year or two. We hope to change this in the near future.

Perl Oasis

We are the proud sponsors of the annual Perl Oasis workshop which has had international attendance for two years running. We're currently in early planning stages for the 2011 Perl Oasis, and are looking for volunteers to help out

Contact Us

You can contact us by showing up at a meeting (once one is scheduled), or by joining our mailing list.

Meetings

Currently we're don't have a scheduled next meeting. We're looking to start having quarterly meetings this fall and are looking for a suitable venue. If you know of someplace with a stage and a liquor license that wouldn't mind being overrun by geeks every three months, please let us know!
